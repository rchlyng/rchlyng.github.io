ABOUT

Hi, I'm Rachel and this website was made for my Activist Website Project. My idea was advocating for free meals for all students enrolled in public school. I was motivated to do my website on this topic because lunch was free for the 2021-2022 school year due to Covid. This was the first time where lunch lines were long and students were motivated to eat. However, I am sure with the removal of free lunch this year, my peers will not get school food anymore.

IMPORTANCE

Meals For Students is bringing awareness to the need for free lunch for all. In California alone, about 60% of students in publiuc school qualify for free/reduced lunch. Communities of color, communities of immigrants, and communities of students that rely on meals provided from school may be denied, are not aware to sign-up to be provided with lunch, or are embarrassed of their financial situation. Regardless of which scenario, all students enrolled in public school should be fed during school hours for free.

TAKE ACTION

✩Encourage those around you to fill out forms to recieve free/reduced lunch. Regardless if they need it or not.

✩Push your school district to fund school meals by contacting adminsitration and the district board.

✩Actually eat school food. If schools see students eating the lunch, there is more of an incentive to make it free.
